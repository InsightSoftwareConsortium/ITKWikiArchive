ITK is 10 Years Old !

Please find in this page how we are celebrating this momentous occasion.

When was ITK Born ?

Two dates are considered to be valid definitions of ITK's birth

  • November 6th 1999: First developers meeting
  • March 2000: First CVS commit

Since we can't wait to party, we will start celebrating with the first date.

Celebration Activities

We will celebrate with the following three community activities

  • Dashboard Fest 1.0
  • Adopt-A-Bug 2.0
  • ITK Stories and anecdotes
  • ITK CVS History
  • Dashboard Fest 2.0
  • ITKv4 Compilers Farewell
