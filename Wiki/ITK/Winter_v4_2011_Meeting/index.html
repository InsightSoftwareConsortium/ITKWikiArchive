Registration Information

Registration is now open for the ITKv4 Winter Meeting.

Breakfast and lunch for both days is included. Dinner is optional and will be planned for Wednesday evening at Boston Beer Works, but is NOT included in the registration fee of $60.

The last day to register for the ITK4 meeting is Wednesday, January 26, 2011.

No refunds will be offered for cancellations after January 26, 2011.

Meeting Room

We will be meeting in the Room 563 in the Warren Alpert Building. (map) . You will need to check in with the security guard (tell her you're here for the ITK meeting) at the entrance and present your ID.
