China Visit 2011

Purpose

Tutorial

  • Tutorial Title: ITKv4: Next Generation of Insight Segmentation and Registration Toolkit
  • Presenter: Xiaoxiao Liu from Kitware Inc.
  • Content
    • History of ITK
    • Open source facilities behind ITK( Git, CDash,Gerrit, Insight Journal)
    • What is new in ITKv4 ?
      • Modularization
      • Registration & Segmentation
      • Bridge OpenCV
      • How to migrate
    • Introduction to Simple ITK
    • How to contribute to ITKv4 (Adopt-A-Bug, Dashboard Fest)
  • Methodology

The tutorial will include hands-on exercises performed on Virtual Machines that contain a pre-installed version of ITK and the related software tools. The tutorial will be delivered in Chinese (Mandarin), the materials will be in English.

  • Shenzhen Key Lab of Neuro--Psychiatric Modulation, Shenzhen Institutes of Advanced Technology, CAS, Shenzhen
