ITK Version 4

This section is an archive for the ITK community of the changes to the Insight Toolkit that were intended for the ITK Version for (ITKv4) effort. This effort officially concluded with the release of ITK 4.2 in July 2012. Items that were planned but not completed often have entries in the ITK issue tracker.

  • Release 4.0 wish list
  • This work is supported by ARRA funding from the NLM.
  • More information is available here: Release 4 Planning.
  • The kick-off meeting for this project took place from June 28-July 2 in Bethesda.
  • We welcome the ITK community to comment on the proposed changes.
  • The Fall v4 2010 Meeting took place November 8-10 in Iowa City.
  • The Winter_v4_2011_Meeting took place on February 2-3 in Boston.
  • The Summer_v4_2011_Meeting took place on June 28-29 in Chapel Hill, North Carolina.
  • The Fall_v4_2011_Meeting took place on September 27-28 at the NLM in Bethesda, MD.
