Overview

This patch release includes fixes to filters that did not take image direction into account.

The modifications were already made in the ITK main CVS trunk, and were back ported to the ITK 3.10 branch.

Many thanks to Hans Johnson (University of Iowa) and Tom Vercauteren (Mauna Kea Technologies) for for their hard work on identifying and fixing these problems.
